Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

At Pexvolt Engineering, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you visit our website, in compliance with Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019.

1. Who We Are

We are Pexvolt Engineering, located in Thika, Kiambu County, Kenya. If you have any questions about your data, you can reach us at pexvoltengineering@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways:

  • Contact Form: When you request a quote or send an inquiry, we collect your name, phone number, and email address.
  • Website Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This data is anonymous and includes things like which pages you visit and how long you stay on the site.

3. Why We Collect This Data

We use your information strictly to:

  • Respond to your service requests and quote inquiries.
  • Improve our website experience based on anonymous usage data.

4. Data Sharing and Retention

We respect your data. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We only keep your contact details for as long as necessary to provide our services to you.

5. Your Rights

Under Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019, you have the right to:

  • Ask us what information we have about you.
  • Request that we correct any wrong information.
  • Ask us to delete your personal data from our records.
  • Withdraw your consent for us to use cookies at any time.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to help Google Analytics function. You can choose to accept or decline these cookies via the banner on our homepage. If you decline, Google Analytics will not be loaded.
